browserlab — in-browser LLM engine comparison

Load a model, run the same prompt through four engines side by side — all client-side, weights fetched from Hugging Face, nothing leaves your tab.

How it works. Each lane loads the model independently into the browser's WebGPU device and runs a greedy decode of your prompt, reporting tokens/sec. Models are fetched from Hugging Face on first use (cached by the browser afterward). The custom WGSL lane is the engine from gemma4-webgpu with the Qwen3 port + in-shader Q4_K/Q8; the others are transformers.js, raw onnxruntime-web, and LocalMind's hand-rolled engines. Part of browser-big-fast-lab.
